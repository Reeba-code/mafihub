Innovating the Future

Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub is at the forefront of technological advancement, empowering communities and businesses through cutting-edge digital solutions and fostering a culture of innovation.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for digital transformation in rural areas, fostering sustainable growth and bridging the technology divide through innovative solutions and community engagement.

Our Mission

To empower rural communities by providing accessible, innovative technology solutions that enhance education, productivity, and quality of life while creating opportunities for economic growth.

Our Approach

We believe in collaborative innovation, working closely with communities, businesses, and government to identify needs and develop sustainable, technology-driven solutions.

Business Goals & Objectives
  • Build collaborative, innovative communities that continually engage in sharing resources and transferring knowledge through technology.
  • Introduce, empower, train, and enable entrepreneurs, aspiring entrepreneurs, and tertiary students in the small business sector to succeed in growing and creating sustainable jobs.
  • Use ICT to facilitate, create, develop, grow, and implement innovative ideas that ultimately bear social and economic benefits for South Africa.
  • Add value to existing companies, foster entrepreneurship and technological transfer, generate knowledge-based jobs, and attract innovative companies and institutions.
  • Provide access to appropriate rental space and flexible leases while sharing business services and equipment.
