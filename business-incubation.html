What is Incubation?

Join our incumbents on their transformative journey from aspiring individuals with a vision to accomplished leaders making real impact.

Entrepreneur Journey Roadmap

Follow our participants' journey from idea to impact with MDiHub's comprehensive incubation program.

Discovery

Participants discover MDiHub, a place where ideas take shape and mentorship lights the path.

In this foundational stage, aspiring entrepreneurs discover the power of proper incubation. They connect with experienced mentors, explore market opportunities, and begin shaping their raw ideas into viable business concepts. The discovery phase is about exploration, learning, and building the foundation for future success.

Growth

With access to skills development, market insights, and shared resources, participants begin building their prototypes.

During the growth phase, entrepreneurs gain access to critical resources including skills development programs, market research, technical support, and shared workspace facilities. This is where ideas transform into tangible prototypes and minimum viable products. Participants learn essential business skills while building and testing their solutions.

Impact

Through guidance and advanced support, participants launch their solutions, creating impact locally and beyond.

The impact stage represents the culmination of the incubation journey. With refined products, validated business models, and strong support networks, entrepreneurs launch their solutions into the market. They create jobs, solve real problems, and generate positive impact in their communities while building sustainable, scalable businesses.

Did You Know?

Fascinating facts about South African entrepreneurship, innovation, and intellectual property. These insights are inspired by the Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub (MDIHUB) and its incubation programs, which foster startups and cultivate future innovators in the region.

Patents in South Africa

The first recorded patent in South Africa was granted in 1859 for a new type of safety lamp. Today, IP protection through patents and trademarks is vital for local startups to scale globally.

Incubation Success

Startups that go through South African business incubators have a 75% survival rate compared to only 40% for others. Programs like those at MDIHub support early-stage ventures and help boost success rates.

SA Innovation Hubs

South Africa has over 60 tech hubs and accelerators, including Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub, which are shaping the next generation of African innovators.

IP Value in SA

Strong trademarks and copyrights in South Africa can significantly increase a company's valuation. Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub (MDIHUB) works with partners such as NIMPO and WIPO to help startups navigate IP protection and maximize their brand value.

Mentorship in SA

Entrepreneurs who join mentorship programs are 5 times more likely to grow their businesses successfully and secure funding locally. At MDIHUB, startups like Key Fertility and Veish Group have benefited from incubation and mentorship through partners such as CSIR, Telkom Future Makers, gaining access to guidance, resources, and networks that help them scale.

Tech Growth SA

South Africas tech sector is booming! Startups incubated at MDIHUB, like Midi Media and Alpha Labs, are leveraging innovative solutions to scale quickly and compete both locally and internationally.

Renewable Energy Innovation

Startups like Mega G incubated at MDIHUB are developing renewable energy solutions, driving sustainable growth and innovation across South Africa.

Award-Winning SA Startups

Incubatees from MDIHUB, like ManSalema and BluDynamix, have won local and international awards, gaining recognition and proving that South African innovation competes on the global stage.
