CALL FOR ENTRIES

ADVANCING CLEANTECH INNOVATION AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP FOR CLIMATE ACTION AND SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT IN SOUTH AFRICA.

MiHub is proud to be a partner in the GCIP 2.0 Hubs and Spokes Initiative

Under the GCIP 2.0 Hubs and Spokes initiative, the Accelerator is being delivered in collaboration with nine cleantech implementing partners across South Africa. These partners include leading innovation hubs, universities, and business incubators that provide tailored acceleration support, expert mentorship, and access to valuable networks and funding opportunities.

MiHub as an implementing partner, will continue to contribute to the advancement of clean technology innovation and entrepreneurship in South Africa.

For Cleantech entrepreneurs & innovators

NORTH-WEST PROVINCE NODE

Eligible Solutions

Solutions should fall within the following sub-disciplines but are not limited to:

  • Renewable energy
  • Energy efficiency
  • Water efficiency
  • Waste beneficiation
  • Green buildings
  • E-mobility/transportation
  • Advanced Eco materials & chemicals
  • Climate-smart Agritech, among others

Pre-Acceleration Objectives

  • Increased participation of under-represented groups.
  • Support the development of new cleantech solutions.
  • Assist entrepreneurs in addressing key transitory gaps.
  • Provide support to a larger more diverse group of entrepreneurs.
