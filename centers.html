Facilities

Transforming North West Province with Innovation and Technology

MDiHub offers state-of-the-art facilities designed to foster innovation and collaboration in the North West Province. Our spaces include modern co-working areas, fully equipped training rooms, high-speed internet access, and prototyping labs with the latest hardware and software tools. Members and visitors can leverage these facilities for workshops, hackathons, incubation programs, and hands-on projects, creating an environment where ideas turn into real-world solutions.

Specialized Zones

Makerspace

A collaborative workspace for making, learning, exploring and sharing that uses high tech to no tech tools. Perfect for hardware development, prototyping and manufacturing.

VR Space

Explore immersive virtual reality experiences, training simulations, and prototyping in a state-of-the-art VR lab.

Auditorium

Large-scale presentation and event space designed for conferences, workshops, and public events.

Meeting Room

Professional meeting spaces equipped with presentation technology, perfect for client meetings, team brainstorming, and collaborative sessions.

Boardroom

High-end boardroom for executive meetings, strategic planning, and decision-making sessions.

Co-Working Space

Open, flexible workspace with hot desks, community areas, and networking opportunities for freelancers and remote workers.

Landing Office Space

Fully-equipped private offices for startups and entrepreneurs needing professional workspace with all amenities included.
