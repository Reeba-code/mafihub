Meet The MiHub Team

Meet our team of dynamic and highly skilled individuals, each bringing unique expertise, creativity, and passion to the table. Together, we blend diverse backgrounds and talents to drive innovation, deliver impactful solutions, and create an environment where collaboration thrives.

Joseph Ndaba
Chief Executive Officer

Joseph Ndaba is the visionary Founder & CEO of Mahikeng Innovation Hub (MIHub) and serves as a Commissioner for South Africa's Presidential Commission on the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR). With a passion for youth development and technology-driven transformation, Ndaba established MIHub to empower local communities through 4IR-enabled entrepreneurship and innovation. Under his leadership, the hub has launched satellite locations across the province and supports prototyping, incubation, hackathons, and skills development for aspiring entrepreneurs.
