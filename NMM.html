The Mafikeng Cisco EDGE centre is a partnership between Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub and Cisco SA.
The Mafikeng Cisco EDGE Centre is an incubation space with a Cisco Networking Academy.
The space is designed to engage the local ICT SMME market and introduce them to the era of the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR) with Cisco as the key enabler. The centre will help these SMMEs scale within their respective disciplines using the Cisco technology stack, with the opportunity to become Cisco partner resellers.

The Cisco EDGE Center is an innovation hub designed to foster technological advancements and digital transformation in various sectors. It serves as a collaborative environment where businesses, startups, and academic institutions come together to leverage Cisco’s cutting-edge technologies and resources.
The Cisco EDGE Center accelerates innovation and helps businesses scale faster in an increasingly digital economy.

  • Training and Enablement Programs – Skill development programs including IT, cybersecurity, and more via Cisco Networking Academy.

Networking Academy Curriculum & Certifications

The Cisco Networking Academy offers a comprehensive curriculum to equip individuals with the skills necessary for a successful career in networking and IT. The curriculum includes:

  • CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate): Learn the fundamentals of networking, IP addressing, and routing.
  • CCNP (Cisco Certified Network Professional): Advanced knowledge of enterprise networking and troubleshooting.
  • CyberOps Associate: A certification focusing on cybersecurity and protecting networks from cyber threats.
  • IoT Fundamentals: Understand the Internet of Things, its architecture, and applications.
  • Cybersecurity Essentials: Learn key cybersecurity concepts and practices to secure networks and devices.
